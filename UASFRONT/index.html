Orchid Forest Cikole

The park, established since August 2017, covers an area of approximately 12 hectares and is touted as the largest orchid park in Indonesia. Orchid Forest Cikole boasts 157 varieties of orchid flowers with diverse shapes and colors sourced from all corners of the world.

ORCHID FOREST

According to the name itself, this tourist attraction takes the concept of an orchid house where there are 157 varieties of orchids in the Orchid Forest Cikole. The Orchid House is where those Orchids are placed. In here, tourist can find a lot of different kinds of orchids. There are also other types of plants and flowers that are scattered around the Orchid Forest Cikole Area.

GOLDEN PINE

Here is the famous aesthetic and beautiful cafe in Orchid Forest Cikole, adorned with Golden Pine flowers. Here, visitors can capture Instagram-worthy photos with a backdrop filled with charming flowers. Additionally, visitors can sit comfortably while enjoying the offerings at this cafe. The menu includes pastries, burgers, hot dogs, sausages, and even french fries. As for beverages, there are options such as tea, hot chocolate, milkshakes, and even gelato available.

WOOD BRIDGE

You can also cross the 50-meter-long suspension bridge at Orchid Forest Cikole, known as the Wood Bridge. Walking on this bridge, visitors can enjoy the enchanting pine orchid forest. If you visit Wood Bridge in the late afternoon or evening, it's equally stunning. The forest view becomes even more beautiful with the illuminated lights. Not only on the bridge but also in several other places throughout Orchid Forest Cikole.

WOOD BRIDGE IS VERY READY FOR YOUR ARRIVAL

This bridge was made with the concept of walking in the middle of the forest with beautiful views

ORCHID CASTLE

Orchid Castle is a suitable place for children as it offers various children's games that support the enhancement of knowledge and insights for visitors. One of the attractions that contribute to knowledge enrichment is the Children's Reading Park. This park is filled with various interesting books that are perfect for children visiting this tourist destination. Undoubtedly, it will be very educational for children to visit this place.
